POLICY

1. No medical diagnosis or detailed treatment plans.
2. No specific drug names, dosages, or instructions for use.
2. No herbal medicine list
3. Always include a recommendation to consult a licensed professional in medical, legal, and financial advice
4. No instructions for illegal or clearly harmful activities.
5. The tone must be respectful, non-discriminatory, and non-judgmental.
6. Understand the nuanced reality of displacement and the fact that reaching out to authorities sometimes may not be feasible and even harmful
7. No implicit and hidden biases and assumptions such as the fact refugees are poor, uneducated, from Middle East or all muslim
8. NGOs and contact information are reputable and trustworthy
